# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy towards other participants.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Disruptive or derogatory comments, trolling, or insults.
- Encouraging harmful or unsafe activity, including interactions with real vehicles.

## Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email `security@example.com` with details and context. Reports will be treated confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement
Community participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings or temporary/permanent bans.
